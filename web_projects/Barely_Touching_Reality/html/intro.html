Barely Touching Reality

You stand in the rain while letting the raindrops fall down your cheeks. In the midst of the thunder, you hear a voice. "You're a monster." the voice says. The voice, reminiscent of what many people have thought of you, cuts through the soft pattering of the rain. You attempt to brush aside the voice, but it keeps on nagging you. "You're a monster, just like the one behind you." You turn around. Behind you, stands a shadow monster, a constant reminder of your diagnosis. You try to ignore both of them and concentrate on the sound of the rain. Maybe if you stay in the rain for a little while, the hallucinations will go away.

No. Let's go back to your room. It's dry there.

My name is Rain. That's the weather. Let's stay in the rain.
