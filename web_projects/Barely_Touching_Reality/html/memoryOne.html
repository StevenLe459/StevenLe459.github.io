Barely Touching Reality

The first time you realized that people thought of you as a monster, you were in math class focusing on a test. In the background, you heard the sound of children laughing. You tried to ignore it, but the eerie nature of the sound eventually broke your concentration. The calculations you were once doing in a breeze became a muddle in your brain. All your thoughts could focus on was a feeling of impending doom. A thought gets implanted into your head. "They're coming for you. For you especially."

After class, you immediately rushed to your friend's side. "They're coming for me!" you blurted out to her. "Wait, what?" she asked. "The laughing children. They're coming after me." Your friend laughs nervously. "You're joking right?" she replied. Your face drops. Why doesn't she believe you? Can't she hear the laughing children too? "Don't you hear the laughing children too?" you ask. Your friend still laughs nervously. "No..." she replies. You realize that only you can hear that eerie laughter. You decide to drop the subject. "Never mind. Let's go to lunch", you tell your friend. As you walk to lunch, you can't help but see that your friend avoids your gaze.

Your friend barely talks to you after that incident.
